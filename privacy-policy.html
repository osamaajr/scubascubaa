scuba Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

scuba is a Chrome extension that detects a simple webcam gesture and plays a local GATO MODE video while the gesture is active.

Data Collection

scuba does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. The extension does not upload camera video, images, audio, or gesture data to any server.

Camera Use

scuba uses your camera only inside the extension popup to detect face and hand landmarks for the gesture. Camera frames are processed locally in Chrome and are not saved.

Remote Code

scuba does not fetch or execute remote code. JavaScript, WebAssembly, MediaPipe models, video, audio, and image assets are packaged with the extension and run locally.

Third Parties

scuba does not share user data with third parties.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the publisher using the contact email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
